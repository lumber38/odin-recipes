Buttered Noodles

A simple recipe for the cultured taste.

Buttered noodles are a simple dish for a refined palete. The ingredients are few but the taste is uncomparable.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 8 oz. Dried Farfalle
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 cloves of chopped garlic (optional)
  • Grated parmesean cheese (to taste)

Steps

  1. Put water in a pot to boil, adding the teaspoon of salt.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the pasta to the pot
  3. Let the pasta boil for approximately 8 minutes, check tenderness to preference
  4. Remove the pasta from the pot, making sure to keep some of the pasta water.
  5. In a pan over medium-low heat, put the butter (optional garlic) in to melt.
  6. Once the butter is melted (and the garlic browned) add the pasta and mix thoroughly.
  7. If the pasta is dry, add a bit of the pasta water to the bowl
  8. Top with parmesean cheese to taste.
